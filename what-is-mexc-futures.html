USDT-M Futures

USDT-Margined (USDT-M) futures contracts are settled and margined in USDT (Tether). Traders open long or short positions using USDT as collateral. These contracts are ideal for traders who prefer stablecoin settlements and want to avoid volatility in margin value.

  • Stable margin (USDT)
  • High liquidity and wide market adoption
  • Supports cross and isolated margin modes
  • Up to 200x leverage on selected pairs

USDC-M Futures

USDC-Margined (USDC-M) futures work similarly to USDT-M, but use USDC (USD Coin) as collateral. This option is suitable for users who prefer USDC for regulatory compliance or platform integration reasons.

  • USDC-backed margining
  • Preferred for DeFi and stablecoin ecosystem users
  • Isolated & cross margin options
  • Efficient for API and institutional trading

COIN-M Futures

COIN-Margined (COIN-M) futures are margined and settled using the cryptocurrency being traded. For example, a BTC/USDT COIN-M future would use BTC as both margin and settlement currency.

  • Earn native token (e.g., BTC or ETH) as PnL
  • Ideal for long-term crypto holders
  • No need to convert to stablecoins
  • Exposure to crypto price appreciation
