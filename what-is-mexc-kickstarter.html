What is MEXC Kickstarter?

MEXC Kickstarter is an innovative token listing and fundraising platform launched by the MEXC cryptocurrency exchange. Through Kickstarter, MEXC users can vote using MX tokens to support promising new tokens. How MEXC Kickstarter Works

  • Voting: Users vote by locking their MX tokens for a specific period.
  • Airdrop Rewards: If the project is successful, participants receive free tokens proportional to their votes.
  • No Token Deduction: Voting does not cost any MX tokens – the locked tokens are returned after the event.
  • Listing: The supported project gets listed on MEXC with full trading functionality.
