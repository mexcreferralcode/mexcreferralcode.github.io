How to Transfer Crypto Between Wallets on MEXC

  1. Log in to your account or register MEXC Exchange.
  2. Navigate to Wallet > Overview.
  3. Click on the Transfer button beside the wallet you want to move funds from.
  4. Select the source and destination wallets (e.g., Spot to Futures).
  5. Enter the amount of crypto you want to transfer.
  6. Click Confirm — your funds will be moved instantly.
